Facts & Figures

2011-12 has been an exhilarating year for IRFT !!!

IRFT 2011-12 MILESTONES -


Ethical Business Solutions (EBS) has successfully:

  • Conducted social compliance audits diversifying into a wide range of product sectors/categories.
  • Delivered training programmes on the code of compliance, broadening its reach to South East Asia.
  • Introduced a new service ‘Continuous Improvement Monitoring’ which aims at achieving Sustainable Compliance, this has been well received and the New Financial Year will see implementation of the same in a wide spectrum of industries.
  • Been working in the unorganized sector with an aim to achieve social compliance that is practical, and achieve long-term improvements in working conditions in such a setting.
  • Conducted various trainings related to Code of Conduct, in the Agriculture sector, looking not only at social compliance but Agronomical aspects as well towards sustained productivity.
  • Partnered in a benchmarking field visit involving a comparative study in global standards for cotton with two multi-stakeholder initiatives.

Fair Trade India created awareness on the concept of Fairness in Trade to:

  • 32,00,000 consumer outreach through Pan India Film Screening on the concept of “ Fairness in Trade”
  • College Outreach through direct awareness building programmes: 2,600 Management students from 20 colleges in Mumbai and 5 colleges in Pune.
  • College outreach through college events: 35,000 students got exposed to the concept through various college activities and events in Mumbai, Pune and Bangalore.
  • 6,200 school students outreach from 5 schools in Mumbai through direct sessions.

Community Business Support (CBS)

  • More than 10 NGOs were given a platform for Market Access through exhibitions in Mumbai and Pune
    • Concern India Mela - Kala Ghoda, Mumbai
    • Mega Trade Fair on Corporate Gifts and Stationery Instruments, Mumbai
    • Urban Haat by CIDCO, Belapur
    • Mastek Foundation, Navi Mumbai
    • Thakur College of Engineering and Technology, Mumbai
    • Christmas Bazaar at Bandra, Mumbai
    • The Vishwa Show, Pune
    • Trifles’12, Thodomal Sahani Engineering College, Mumbai
  • CBS initiated Livelihood intervention with the ‘women under trials’ of Byculla Jail in Mumbai.
  • Livelihood workshop was conducted at ‘MNB Industrial Home for the Blind’.
  • CBS actively participated at the state level Marketing Workshop on Handicrafts of Maharashtra State.
  • For enhancing sustainable corporate procurement, initial orders of products were placed by a 5 Star Hotel and a School in Mumbai.
  • ‘Humanity Partnership’ with St. Stanislaus High School for environment friendly “No Plastic Campaign”. It is the first school to adopt the concept of ‘Fairness in Trade’ and include it in the school’s curriculum.
  • Facilitated the Fair Trade Certification process of Om Creation Trust.

IRFT had interns from educational institutes of India, Japan and Canada.
