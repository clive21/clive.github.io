IRFT welcomes volunteers to support its activities. Individuals who believe in sustainable development with a background in the social sector, agriculture, environment, marketing, designing and capacity building are welcome to work with IRFT as volunteers.

IRFT is an equal opportunity employer. Candidates who have a background and expertise in the social sector, agriculture, environment, marketing, designing and capacity building are especially encourage to join us.

Please send your CVs to admin@irft.org or to apply online and we will get back to you if there are any vacancies.

Please note: All vacancies are subjected to availability and may include travel.
