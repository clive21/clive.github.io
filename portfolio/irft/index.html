IRFT on board with Indian Institute of Corporate Affairs (IICA) in the Implementing Agencies Hub!

A rigorous due diligence process was adopted by IICA in order to prepare a database of credible implementing agencies across India.
With great pride we announce that the database is ready and IRFT - International Resources for Fairer Trade has been included in the first list of the credible NGOs by IICA.
Check us out on: http://iahub.iica.in/DatabaseReport.aspx

IRFT has been selected in the first lot of 47 credible NGOs , of which 8 are based in Maharashtra. This list is cleared by seven ministries, including the Home Ministry and the Intelligence Bureau.
